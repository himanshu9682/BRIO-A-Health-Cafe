Who we are?

BRIO – A Health Café is India’s first-ever health café brand, founded in 2020 with an inspiring mission: To make India strong in health.

We are committed to serving the best quality of food product by providing best in nutrition from fresh fruits, vibrant vegetables, and other healthy food ingredients. At BRIO, we blend the wisdom of Ayurveda with modern techniques to create food that fuels your body and nurtures your well-being—without ever compromising on nutritional value.

Our Presence

BRIO is growing with the youth of India—right where ideas spark and futures are built.

We proudly serve in four leading institutions: PSIT, RAMA University, AXIS Institute, and AKGEC, where we’ve become a daily part of student life. From early morning snacks to mid-day power bowls, from evening light food to night milk routine, we’re there to fuel learning, conversations, and dreams.

Being wellness partner in above college campuses, we have significantly reduced the consumption of junk food, sugary beverages, and refined flour-based items by providing healthy meal choices.

Less junk, more joy—every day.

Our Life, Our Philosophy <3

Our Purity Promise

Zero Compromise

At BRIO, we champion a philosophy of clean, conscious nourishment. Our offerings are free from adulterants, artificial colors, flavors, and preservatives—ensuring that only nature’s finest touches your plate.

We preserve the nutritional integrity of every ingredient through thoughtful processing and uphold the highest standards of hygiene, water safety, and sustainability.

Our Commitment

Clean Culture

At BRIO, hygiene and cleanliness are not just practices—they are principles we live by. From sourcing ingredients to preparing your food, every step is carried out in sanitized, controlled environments using “state-of-the-art” equipment and food-grade processes.

We ensure uncompromised product quality, backed by the high standards in hygiene and nutrition.

We are equally committed to the planet. That’s why we use environmentally friendly disposables and follow “a low or no-plastic policy” across all operations.

Every meal you enjoy at BRIO is crafted with care—for your health and for the Earth.

Our Sustainability Model

VRIO

At BRIO, we align our practices with the VRIO sustainability model—focusing on long-term Value, Rarity, Imitability, and Organization.

This helps us deliver not just healthful products, but a resilient, responsible brand that stands the test of time.
